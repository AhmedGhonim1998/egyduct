About Egy Duct

Certified Precision.
Global Partnerships.

Egy Duct Corporate was founded in 2017 by a group of HVAC experts with the mission of improving the health and comfort of indoor spaces. We provide complete solutions for ventilation problems arising from air & sound — including Ventilation Fans, Air Conditioners, Smoke Management, Sheet Metals, HVAC Duct Works, Dampers, Diffusers, Grills, Louvers, and all HVAC Accessories.

Our Vision

To be a leader in the HVAC industry, known for our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction — creating a world where everyone can breathe easily, with access to clean and healthy air.

Our Mission

To introduce and provide the latest technologies with high-quality products tailored to our customers' unique needs, while prioritizing value, energy efficiency, and environmental sustainability within the provided budget.
